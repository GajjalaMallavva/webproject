BEST WATERFALLS IN CHHATTISGARH

Chitrakot waterfalls

Chitrakot waterfall is Chhattisgarh's largest and most water-logged waterfall. It is considered as the main waterfall of Bastar division. The waterfall is located at a distance of 40 km from Jagdalpur and 273 km from Raipur . Chitrakote waterfall is Chhattisgarh's largest and most water-logged waterfall. It is considered as the main waterfall of Bastar division. Due to being adjacent to Jagdalpur, it has also gained fame as a major picnic spot. Due to the similar shape of horse feet , this fall is also called Niagara of India. Chitrakoot waterfall is very beautiful and the tourists like it very much. A large water body falling in the middle of the strong trees and Vindhya ranges, which falls from this water fall, attracts tourists' attention.

Tirathgarh waterfalls

The falls comes under the eco-tourism venture of the state tourism department as it enjoins the Kanger National Park. The fall here splits into multiple falls, creating a stunning vista. The waterfall has lush green forest surrounding it. Well maintained steps lead down to the bottom. It is located at a distance of 35 kilometres (22 mi) south-west of Jagdalpur. One can approach the falls from Darbha, near state highway that connects Jagdalpur to Sukma. One has to take a jeep at Darbha junction to visit Teerathgarh and Kutumsar. Kutumsar Caves and Kailash Gufa are nearby attractions. It is in Kanger Ghati National Park.

Ghatarani waterfalls

Ghatarani Waterfalls is located around 80 km from Raipur and is the largest in Chhattisgarh. It is surrounded by lush greenery and is a popular picnic spot. You can reach this breathtaking sight by taking a small trek through the forest. At the bottom of the waterfall, there is a naturally formed pool where you can swim and play with your family.
