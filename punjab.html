BEST WATERFALLS IN PUNJAB

Bhagsu Nag waterfalls

Bhagsunag Falls, also known as Bhagsu Falls, is a beautiful waterfall situated in the village of Bhagsu behind Bhagsunath Temple, near McLeod Ganj in Himachal Pradesh district of Kangra. A delightful 2-kilometre trek from McLeod Ganj leads you to the quaint Bhagsu village. Situated 7000 feet above sea level, it is just the beginning of the high-altitude mountain range. The majestic Bhagsu waterfall is a sight to behold.

Jogini waterfalls

This is one of the top attractions you must include in Manali Packages. The flow of the water is heaving in monsoons and suggested not to adventure into the water during this time. Jogini Mata temple is at the base of the waterfall.

Jana waterfalls

Jana Fall is located in a small town Jana, which is a few km away from Naggar in Manali. The name of the waterfall, Jana waterfall came from the name of the villageJana Waterfall lies in Jana Village, 35km away from Manali, in Naggar. It is a spot where you can try local delicacies like siddu. You will also find apple orchards here.
