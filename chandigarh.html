BEST WATERFALLS IN CHANDIGARH

Chadwick waterfalls

Chadwick Waterfalls in Chandigarh is a great tourist destination and a popular weekend getaway in the vicinity. This stunning waterfall is just 112 km away from Chandigarh where people go to enjoy picnics and lovely day trips. The forceful water and gigantic rock formation will astonish you with their majestic charm. Bhagsunag waterfalls

Bhagsunag is another one among the popular waterfalls near Chandigarh amidst the serene nature. Bhagsunag Waterfall is situated at a great height from the sea level so tourists can enjoy a soothing climate here. During monsoons, the waterfall becomes fuller and more beautiful with natural abundance. Altogether, the waterfall site is a prime destination for exploring nature, trekking, and enjoying family picnics amidst a refreshing natural setting. More Details: Height of Waterfall: 7000 feet Entry Fee: No entry fee Timing: Throughout the day How to Reach: Cab or taxi from Chandigarh Best Time to Visit: September to November Things to Do: Picnic, trekking, rock climbing, nature walk Ideal trip duration: 3 to 4 hours

Chhoie waterfalls

Chhoie Waterfall is a pristine tourist destination located in Tirthan Valley. Chhoie Fall is surrounded by many other small to big waterfalls so, people can enjoy a great day trip exploring them. Besides, the hilly landscape has made this place an adobe for trekking lovers. In addition, people can also go hiking in the nearby forests. More Details: Height of Waterfall: 30 feet Entry Fee: No entry fee Timing: Throughout the day How to Reach: Cab or taxi from Chandigarh Best Time to Visit: June to September Things to Do: Trekking, hiking, nature walk Ideal trip duration: 3 to 4 hours
