BEST WATERFALLS IN ARUNACHAL PRADESH

Nuranang waterfalls

Nuranang Falls (also known as Jang Falls & Bong Bong Falls ), 100 m high waterfall, is located 2 km northeast of the Jang town on the motorable Jang Falls Road in the Tawang district of Arunachal Pradesh state of India. Jang is 40 km southeast of Tawang, north of Sela Tunnel, and between Tawang and Bomdila on NH-13 Trans-Arunachal Highway. Jang Hydal Plant, near the base of fall, is a small hydel plant which generates electricity for local use

Bap Teng Kang waterfalls

A stunning waterfall, Bap Teng Kang is amongst the best places to see near Tawang. Situated in Yumkhang, some 85 kms from Tawang on the route to Zemithang, the waterfall has become quite popular as a picnic spot, where people can enjoy swimming and playing with water. Also known as BTK Falls, it is a 30 m high cascade that is surrounded by lush greenery which adds to its beauty.

Sirki waterfalls

Sirki Waterfall is located at a distance of approx 10 kms from Pasighat, it is a must visit to enjoy adventurous trek and witness the marvel of nature. The Sirki Waterfalls is about 45 meters high and the turbulent water comes gushing down year-round, especially in the rainy season. Apart from witnessing the majestic appearance of the Sirki waterfall, there are many adventure activities you can head for as well.
