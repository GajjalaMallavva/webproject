BEST WATERFALLS IN UTTAR PRADESH

Devdari waterfalls

The crystal clear waters, gurgling over the rocks present a breathtaking sight. The scenic, tranquil surroundings lend a sense of mysticism to the experience. People usually come here for a day trip and though there is no accommodation here, there are several eateries here to serve snacks and drinks.
