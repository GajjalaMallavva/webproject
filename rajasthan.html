BEST WATERFALLS IN RAJASTHAN

Gaipernath waterfalls

Gaipernath Waterfall situated in Kota is one of the beautiful destinations bo Rajasthan. The waterfall is available only during the monsoons, and the other times it is dried up. It is approximately about 300 steps trek to the waterfalls. There is a temple named Gaipernath Temple located at the waterfalls. The waterfalls looks breathtaking during monsoons.

Garbhaji waterfalls

Situated close the city of Alwar, Garbhaji Falls is a waterfall and a popular tourist attraction. The waterfalls plunge into a pool making its way through the rocky terrain. Set amidst the jungle, these waterfalls are extremely breath-taking during the monsoon months.

Damoh waterfalls

Damoh, a beautiful waterfall in Sarmathura, is one of the major tourist attractions in the district. The streams in this waterfall are known to remain dry throughout the year, except from July to September when embellish their surroundings with greenery and a variety of fauna.
