BEST WATERFALLS IN NAGALAND

Triple waterfalls

The Triple Falls in Dimapur is a popular natural attraction located in Nagaland, India. It is a cascading waterfall known for its three-tiered formation, which gives it the name 'Triple Falls'. The Triple Falls is situated in Seithekima Village, around 20 kilometers from Dimapur, the largest city in Nagaland.

Phek waterfalls

Phek Waterfall is one of most beautiful tourist attractions of Nagaland if you are an admirer of nature. In the lap of green forest and woods, you will find the Phek Waterfall flowing through it. This is one of the places to visit with family and kids although little trekking is required to reach this attraction.
