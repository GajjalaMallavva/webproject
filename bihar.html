BEST WATERFALLS IN BIHAR

Kakolat waterfalls

Kakolat Waterfall is a picturesque waterfall in Nawada district, popular with tourists due to its scenic surroundings. The falls also play a role in Hindu mythology, when according to legend an ancient king was turned into a python by a rishi's curse and lived within the falls. Kakolat is situated on the southern border of Bihar, and is 33 km from Nawada and 4 km from Thali Bazar. Kakolat waterfall cascades approximately 160 feet (49m), and creates a natural reservoir at its base.It is located near Govindpur police station and is surrounded by forests.

Tons waterfalls

Tons Falls, also known as Tons Nadi Falls, is a magnificent waterfall in West Champaran, Bihar's beautiful and remote district. The falls are situated near the Indo-Nepal border, in a dense forest area surrounded by the foothills of the Himalayas.
