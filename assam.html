BEST WATERFALLS IN ASSAM

Kakochang waterfalls

Kakochang waterfall is also a well-known tourist attraction/picnic spot near Kaziranga. The remains of the historical Numaligarh can also be viewed from this place. The waterfall is located about 13 km from the Bokakhat, a place in the Jorhat Region of Assam. Situated between the Kaziranga National Park and Bokakhat, it is a prominent local eat-outside picnic spot. Few of the famous attractions nearby this waterfall are ruins of Numaligarh, ruins of Deoparbat or Deopahar, landscapes of tea, coffee, and rubber farms.

Panimur waterfalls

The Panimur waterfall is a beautiful waterfall located in the Panimur area under the Dimahasao District of Assam near Haflong and is 215 km away from Assam. The nearest railway station is the New Haflong railway station.

Bhelughat waterfalls

Bhelughat Waterfall is a scenic waterfall in the Udalguri Assam, India, district. The waterfall is known for its picturesque beauty and is a popular destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. The waterfall is surrounded by lush green forests, making it an ideal spot for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful and relaxing getaway.
