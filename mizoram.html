BEST WATERFALLS IN MIZORAM

Tuirihiau waterfalls

Tuirihiau Falls Falls is the most spectacular of all the waterfalls and cascades in the fast flowing rivers of Mizoram. It is located in Vanva river near Buangpui. The uniqueness of this waterfall is that there are caves behind the waterfalls and you see the falls from behind.

Khawhpawp waterfalls

The Khawh Pawp waterfall is located in Aizawl in the Indian state of Mizoram. A strong water current runs through a hole in the rock which has created this spectacular waterfall. The route to this waterfall is a bit rough and confusing. After reaching the place, one needs to walk for a distance of 500 m.

Azuali waterfalls

Azuali Waterfalls is a stunning cascade of water that falls from a height of around 10 meters, creating a cool mist and a soothing sound that attracts visitors from all over the country.
