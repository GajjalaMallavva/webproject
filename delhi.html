BEST WATERFALLS IN DELHI

Neer Garh waterfalls

Neer Garh Waterfall is a beautiful narrow stream of clear water cascading down a rocky terrain cutting through a dense green forest. Also known as 'Neer Gaddu', it has a beautiful jade-blue hue. The waterfall is two-tiered and has two bathing pools.

Patna waterfalls

Patna Waterfall is named after the small village where this waterfall is located. This waterfall is also famous for the limestone caves located next to the waterfall.

Corbett waterfalls

Corbett Falls is a scenic water fall located at 25 kms from Ramnagar. Developed as a picnic spot for tourist, Corbett Water Falls is surrounded by dense teak wood forest which extends its natural beauty. Beautiful place.
